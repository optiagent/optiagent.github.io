How It Works

Five steps. Built for real estate.
Zero leads left behind.

From the moment a lead enters to a booked appointment — every step is tracked, automated, and accounted for.

01
Lead Enters the System

Website form, inbound call, referral, ad response, or manual entry. Every single lead from every source flows into one unified pipeline automatically. Nothing falls through the cracks.

02
Instant Follow-Up Fires

Email and SMS responses trigger immediately while intent is still highest. Speed to lead is protected from the very first minute — no manual intervention required.

03
CRM Staging & Full Team Visibility

Every lead moves through defined stages. Your entire team sees exactly where each prospect stands — eliminating confusion, duplication, and dropped balls.

04
Booking Flow & Reminder Automation

Interested prospects are pushed into appointment booking with automated multi-touch reminders that dramatically increase show rate and recover no-shows before they happen.

05
Weekly Reporting & Accountability

Activity summaries, appointment counts, and full pipeline movement delivered weekly. You always know what's working, what needs attention, and where growth is coming from.
